About Alpha Stay-Lodge

Your Sustainable Retreat

Founded in 2010, Alpha Stay-Lodge was born from a passion for sustainable tourism and a deep respect for nature. Our eco-resort combines luxury with environmental responsibility, creating a unique experience for our guests.

We've implemented numerous green initiatives including solar power, water recycling, and locally-sourced organic food. Our architecture blends seamlessly with the natural surroundings, offering stunning views while minimizing environmental impact.

Our team of hospitality professionals is dedicated to making your stay memorable while preserving the beauty of our planet for future generations.
